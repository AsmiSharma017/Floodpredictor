Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main causes of floods in Himachal Pradesh?
Heavy monsoon rains, cloudbursts, rapid snowmelt, and landslides blocking rivers are the main causes of flooding in Himachal Pradesh.
How does this website predict floods?
Our AI model analyzes rainfall, river data, and historical flood patterns to estimate flood risk for your area in real time.
Where can I get live flood alerts?
You can get live alerts from the IMD Flood Warning Portal and Google Flood Hub.
