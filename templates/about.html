About Us

We are passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems. Our flood prediction system uses artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) to analyze rainfall and weather data, helping communities prepare for floods.

How the Data is Trained

  • We collected historical flood and rainfall data for different regions.
  • We trained a machine learning model to recognize patterns in the data that signal flood risk.
  • The model was integrated into this website, so anyone can enter new weather data and get instant flood risk predictions.

AI-driven flood prediction is transforming disaster preparedness worldwide, making our communities safer and more resilient.
